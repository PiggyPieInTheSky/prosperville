Better Money Habits learning tip #1: Why is the time value of money important to my financial health and long-term financial goals?

The famous physicist Albert Einstein once said about compound interest: “Compound interest is the eighth wonder of the world. He who understands it, earns it; he who doesn't, pays it”

If you learn nothing else from this game, learn the time value of money and the magic of compounding. Learning this when you are young and investing for your future early is a huge opportunity and advantage.
